FIGHTBOYS

The positioning of this film is primarily focused on campus, action, science fiction, and drama. The main content narrates the story of Jack, who feels listless every day, with his life, studies, and interpersonal relationships in disarray, relying solely on games to distract himself.

Everything begins to change after he meets Mike. The teacher Mike introduces Jack to an underground organization on campus - the Fight Club. Jack trains hard there, defeating one opponent after another, ultimately overcoming the demons that haunt him, as well as conquering his own inner lethargy.

The conclusion reveals that Mike is actually another persona of Jack, and Jack himself is the leader. The entire process is a gradual reconciliation with oneself.

CREATIVE IDEA

The focus of this film is an underground fighting club located indoors. To create this atmosphere, we chose to shoot at night, and the selection of a dormitory was made to emphasize the underground characteristics of a secret society where individuals vent their repressed desires.

By dimming the illumination and turning off the ceiling lights, a strong sense of darkness is achieved. Additionally, we use neon lights to create a dual atmosphere of passion and existence within the dimness.

A main light is installed at the top to accentuate the contours of the characters. Yellow lighting serves as supplementary lighting.
