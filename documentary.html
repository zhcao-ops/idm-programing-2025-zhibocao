DOCUMENTARY

The micro-documentary 'Starlight' showcases the efforts and perseverance of three ordinary fitness enthusiasts as they pursue dual transformations of body and mind through authentic fitness stories. It explores their emotional journeys, challenges, and achievements throughout the fitness process, as well as how fitness has changed their lives. The emotional elements embodied in 'Starlight' reveal its role in conveying fitness concepts and promoting nationwide physical fitness.
