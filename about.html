ABOUT ALLEN

I have studied photography and videography, computer image processing, script writing, and visual color grading. My works explore human emotions, creative storytelling, and artistic visual language.

From planning, scripting, shooting, editing to publicity. Each session has its own unique insights and creative thinking. I am committed to combining the aesthetics of cinema with pop culture, traditional Chinese culture, and the art style of painting artists.

I aim to combine film, design, and art in a way that expresses authentic visual storytelling.
